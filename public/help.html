Frequently Asked Questions

To search for properties, go to the Properties page and use the search filters. You can filter by location, price range, number of bedrooms, and more. You can also use the map view to see properties in specific areas.

To list your property, sign up for an owner account and go to your dashboard. Click on "Add New Property" and fill out the form with details about your property, including photos, description, and rental terms. Once submitted, our team will review your listing before it goes live.

We accept various payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Once you've found a property you're interested in, you can contact the owner directly through our messaging system. Click on the "Contact Owner" button on the property details page to send a message. The owner will receive a notification and can respond to your inquiry.
